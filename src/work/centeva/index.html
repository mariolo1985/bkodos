OX Sports
Role: UX Designer
Operations Exchange is a website and app built for better managing sports teams. I worked with a wonderful team, who helped me improve my design skills and better understand the importance of working as a team to accomplish a goal. Also, I learned how to deliver on my part of the design process and complete deliverables on time and with good quality.
This project provided me with the opportunity to...
  • Collaborate designs with a large group.
  • Collaborate with an overseas development team.
  • Brainstorm ideas by creating user flows and user story maps.
  • Ideate by sketching and wireframing ideas.
  • Implement styles to project by using a style guide.
  • Update app components and create new ones.
  • Create important components for the overall project such as the Calendar, Season Schedule, Playbook, and Notebook.
  • Design for multi-platforms; App, Website, and Tablet.
Technology Used: Sketch, Invision, Adobe CC, Dropbox, Jira, Skype, Teams.
